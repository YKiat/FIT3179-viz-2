Suicide Cases from 1985-2016

The topic of this visualisation is about the suicides statistic in 100 countries, from 1985 to 2016. We will be looking on the distribution of suicides cases over different countries, and what causing the people choose to suicides

Suicides Cases By Country and GDP

From the bubble plot, we can tell that United States and Russian has the most suicides cases. One thing to note that which is even the Africa Countries having lower GDP, but their rate of suicide is lower than the country having high GDP such as US. Meanwhile, Japan also have very high number of suicide cases despite of having high GDP.

Suicide Cases by Generation

We notice that the number of suicide cases for Generation X has peaked around the year 2014. The suicide cases have been increasing substantially for the Millenials but it has decreased after 2014. As for generation Boomers, the suicide cases are the highest during the year 1996. Besides that, the number of suicide cases has started decreasing after the year 1990 for generation Silent.

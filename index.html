Suicide Cases from 1985-2016

The topic of this visualisation is about the suicides statistic in 100 over countries, from 1985 to 2016. We will be looking on the distribution of suicides cases over different countries, and the other factors such as gender, age, generation

Suicides Cases By Country and GDP

From the bubble plot, we can tell that United States and Russian has the most suicides cases. One thing to note that which is even the Africa having lower GDP, but their rate of suicide is lower than the country having high GDP such as United States. Meanwhile, Japan also have very high number of suicide cases despite of having high GDP.

Suicide Cases by Generation

We notice that the number of suicide cases for Generation X highest at 2014. The suicide cases have been increasing substantially for the Millenials since year 2000. The suicide cases for Boomer is peak at 1994 and decreased a lot after 2009 Besides that, the number of suicide cases Silent start decreasing at year 2010. Lastly, the number of suicide cases for the Generation Z is low compared to other generation as Generation Z range from 1997 to 2014.

Suicides Cases By Age

Male contribute to a higher number of suicides cases as compared to female. We notice that the group with the most number of suicide cases for male is 25-34 years, while for female is 35-54 years, which shows that people who usually commit suicides is in their middle age. In terms of the total number of suicides, we can see that there is a huge difference for male and female whereby male is having 5188910, female is having 1559510.
